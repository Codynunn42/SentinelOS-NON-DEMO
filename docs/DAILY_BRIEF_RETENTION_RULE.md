# Daily Brief Retention Rule

## Approval Badge

`[APPROVED:MAPPING]`

## Rule

Daily briefs are immutable historical snapshots unless an explicitly named status report supersedes them.

Similarity between daily briefs is not evidence of duplication and does not authorize merge, deletion, archive, or rewrite.

## Governance Reason

Daily briefs preserve operating chronology, deployment facts, proof signals, and decision history. They should remain reviewable even when later reports summarize or supersede part of their content.

## Next Step

If a daily brief is superseded, add a note pointing to the superseding report. Do not remove or merge the original brief.
